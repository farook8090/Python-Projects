Customer Sentiment Analysis Report

iPhone 15 (128GB) · Flipkart

Summary of insights derived from 300 Flipkart customer reviews (ratings, review text) processed for polarity/subjectivity.

1) Overview of the Data Collection & Cleaning

  • Source: Flipkart product reviews for iPhone 15 128GB (300 reviews captured).
  • Fields scraped: Username, review text, star rating.
  • Cleaning: Normalized column types, removed special characters/emoji from text, ensured equal lengths, handled missing values, converted ratings to numeric.
  • Sentiment engine: TextBlob polarity (−1 to +1) & subjectivity (0 to 1).
Note: This report summarizes patterns visible across the 300-review sample.

2) Sentiment Analysis Results

Ratings Snapshot

  • 5★: Dominant share (very positive)
  • 4★: Moderate share (minor issues)
  • ≤3★: Small share (battery/heating/60Hz)

Sentiment Scores

  • Average polarity: High positive (most reviews between ~0.6 and 1.0)
  • Average subjectivity: High (~0.7–1.0) → opinion-heavy, experience-based

Theme Highlights

Positive
  • Excellent camera quality (photos & video)
  • Premium, light design; great in-hand feel
  • Smooth performance (A16 Bionic)
  • Display quality & Dynamic Island usefulness
  • Apple ecosystem (continuity, reliability)
Negative
  • Battery drains faster for heavy users
  • Heating during charging, gaming, long camera use
  • 60Hz refresh rate feels dated at this price

3) Insights

  • Camera as the hero: Most frequently praised aspect; users cite sharper 48MP shots, strong portrait/cinematic video.
  • Perceived value: Many upgraders (from Android/iPhone 11–13) report clearly better performance and display brightness.
  • Friction points: Battery & thermal behavior constrain some heavy-use scenarios; 60Hz refresh rate is a recurring critique.
  • Expectation setting matters: When buyers know the 60Hz/battery trade-offs, satisfaction remains high due to camera/performance.

4) Recommendations

Product Improvements (Apple)

  • Further optimize battery longevity and charging efficiency.
  • Improve thermal management for camera/gaming sessions.
  • Consider 90–120Hz display to match segment expectations.

Marketing Focus (Flipkart)

  • Lead with camera excellence, premium design, and smooth performance.
  • Showcase real customer visuals/testimonials in PDP creatives.
  • Set clear expectations on refresh rate & typical battery life.
  • Promote trusted delivery, open-box checks, and exchange/bank offers.
